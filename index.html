In Memory of, Our Lost and Beloved George

Our Purpose


We want to tell the story of George Sheppard. He died on April 16th 2022 due to an overdose of stress caused by his upcoming IS 201 project. George was many wonderful things. He was kind, he was funny, he could do a one and a half, and a double off of the diving board, however he was also a procrastinator. He waited too long to start on his final website and the stress of getting it done on the last day literally killed him. He was too young to die. This site was created to carry on his legacy and tell his story. We hope that by doing this we can help to keep alive a young soul who left us far too soon.
